ABOUT

COVID-Detect is an AI-powered system designed for the rapid and accurate diagnosis of COVID-19 using chest X-ray images. Traditional diagnostic methods, such as RT-PCR tests and manual radiographic analysis, often come with challenges—long processing times, high costs, and the potential for human error. Our AI-based solution addresses these issues by automating the detection process using deep learning, specifically convolutional neural networks (CNNs).


Why AI Over Traditional Methods?


  1. Speed & Efficiency: Unlike RT-PCR tests, which take hours or even days for results, COVID-Detect can provide real-time diagnosis from chest X-rays in seconds.


  2. Cost-Effectiveness: AI-driven analysis reduces the dependency on expensive laboratory tests and minimizes the need for highly specialized radiologists.


  3. Accuracy & Consistency: Manual diagnosis is prone to subjectivity and fatigue-induced errors, whereas our model—CovNet—delivers consistent and data-driven results with high precision.


  4. Scalability: In resource-limited settings, where access to testing kits and radiologists is restricted, AI-powered tools can provide rapid mass screening, aiding in early detection and containment.


  5. Improved Decision-Making: By integrating AI with medical imaging, COVID-Detect enables healthcare professionals to make quicker and more informed decisions, improving patient outcomes.


With a dataset of over 21,000 labeled X-ray images, including COVID-19, Normal, Pneumonia, and Lung Opacity cases, our CovNet model outperforms traditional architectures like ResNet-50 and VGG-16, ensuring superior classification accuracy.


Our mission is to bridge the gap between technology and healthcare, offering a reliable, fast, and scalable solution for diagnosing COVID-19 and other respiratory diseases. COVID-Detect is not just a tool—it’s a step toward revolutionizing medical diagnostics and making healthcare more accessible worldwide.
